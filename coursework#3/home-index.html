I scream for ice cream

Welcome to our horrifying ice cream store I Scream. It was founded in 1958 by Bob Bobson, also called Spooky Bob. He decided to start this store because of his love for horror and ice cream. All the ice cream is made from secret scary recipes that Spooky Bob and his wife, Creepy Claire, made. Here you can find a bunch of deliciosly scary flavors. So come visit us for a yummy experince.
